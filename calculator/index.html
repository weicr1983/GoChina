How we calculate

We use 2025–2026 averages from Booking.com, Trip.com, and 12306 for high-speed rail. For travelers above 1, hotels are billed per room (1 room per 2 people); food, transport, attractions and rail tickets scale per person. Shopping and other costs are entered as per-person amounts and multiplied by your group size.

FX rate: 1 USD = 7.20 CNY (updated 2026-01-15). Actual bank rates will vary by 1–3%. Peak season (Chinese New Year, Golden Week) prices can rise 30–50%.
