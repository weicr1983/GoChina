144 vs 240 hour visa-free transit

Both policies let you enter China without applying for a visa in advance. The difference is how long you can stay and how widely you can travel.

Side-by-side

144h 240h
Stay length6 days10 days
CoverageSingle designated region20+ provinces, cross-region
Best forShort city breaksMulti-city itineraries
Approval~5–10 min at airport~5–15 min at airport
DocumentsPassport, onward ticketPassport, onward ticket, hotels

How to apply at the airport

  1. 01 Arrive at an eligible international airport with your passport, onward ticket, and (if needed) a visa for your third destination.
  2. 02 At the transit counter, request the 144 / 240-hour visa-free transit permit. Border officers verify your documents in 5–15 minutes.
  3. 03 Collect your permit and enter China. Keep the paper permit with you throughout your stay — hotels will request it at check-in.

Practical tips

  • — Departure must be from the same or an eligible port in the same region.
  • — Your onward ticket must go to a third country / region, not back to your origin.
  • — Hotel staff are required to register foreign guests — carry your passport at all times.
  • — If you enter under 240h, you can now move between many adjacent provinces.
