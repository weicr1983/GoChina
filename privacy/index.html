Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-15

1. Overview

GoChina Trip ("we", "our", "us") is an independent, ad-supported travel guide for foreigners visiting China under the 144 / 240-hour visa-free transit policy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

2. Information we collect

Information you don't give us. You can browse the entire site without creating an account, signing up, or sharing any personal information. The budget calculator runs entirely in your browser; inputs are not sent to any server.

Information collected automatically. When you visit, our hosting provider (Cloudflare) and our analytics provider may record:

  • — IP address (anonymized in analytics)
  • — Browser type and version
  • — Pages visited and time on page
  • — Referring website
  • — Country / region (derived from IP)

3. Cookies and consent

We display a cookie banner on your first visit. You can choose one of three options:

  • — Accept all — enables Google Analytics 4 and (when available) Google AdSense / advertising cookies.
  • — Analytics only — enables Google Analytics 4 only. No advertising cookies.
  • — Essential only — no analytics, no ads. The site still works fully. Only your language preference and consent state are stored in your browser.

We implement Google Consent Mode v2: by default every consent signal (analytics_storage, ad_storage, ad_user_data, ad_personalization) is set to denied until you make a choice, regardless of which option you pick. No analytics or ad data leaves your browser until you actively grant it.

4. Advertising (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display ads. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Some of our other advertising partners and vendors include:

  • — Google AdSense / Ad Manager — Privacy · How Google uses information

5. Analytics (Google Analytics 4)

If you grant analytics permission, we use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand aggregate traffic patterns. Specifically, we measure:

  • — Page views and time on page
  • — Browser locale (to validate our 10-language site)
  • — City page visits (to know which guides get read most)
  • — Calculator and planner usage (anonymous, no inputs recorded)
  • — Country (derived from anonymized IP)

What we do NOT do. We do not use GA4 to identify individual users. We do not record the numbers you enter in the budget calculator. We do not use GA4 to target advertising.

Consent Mode v2. The analytics_storage signal is sent on every page load. Until you grant permission, GA4 receives a denied signal and does not record the visit. We also enable IP anonymization (anonymize_ip: true) and ads_data_redaction as a safety net. See Google's Consent Mode documentation for details.

You may also install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on to block GA4 across all sites.

6. Children

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Data retention

Analytics data is retained for 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted by Google. Ad personalization is governed by your Google account settings.

8. Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you are in the EU / UK / California, you have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. Since we do not collect personal data directly, most requests should be directed to Google via your Google account. For everything else, contact us at hello@gochinatrip.com.

9. Changes

We will update this page and change the "Last updated" date if our practices change. Material changes will be flagged on the homepage for 30 days.
